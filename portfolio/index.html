Portfolio

Financial Application

Mockup (in development)

This project is the total redesign of a white-label banking application with millions of users. The old site is 10+ years old and in dire need of an update. We used client and user feedback to create two templates, both of them responsive designs. A configuration screen allows the admins to set colors and labels, while a separate customer service screen facilitates quick resolution of customer issues. It is built using Angular.js and C#.

Tools and software: LESS, Angular.js, Visual Studio, and SMACSS principles

revenueanalytics.com

Lake Union Charters and Adventures

Sailboat charter and lesson company out of Seattle

lakeunioncharters.com

Geoff at LUCA was referred to me through a friend. His old site was in an old install of Drupal, with a very dark (not very adventurous) theme. We moved his site to WordPress and added a few custom post types and custom meta boxes for his charters, lessons, boats, etc., which made it easy for him and his team to update the site while maintaining consistency throughout.

Tools and software: WordPress, Sublime Text 3, SASS, Gulp
